Sandia Labs: Counterfeit Bolt Detection

Detecting counterfeit fasteners using imaging and machine learning.

Overview

Counterfeit bolts pose serious risks in aerospace, defense, and industrial supply chains. They can look identical to authentic parts but often fail due to poor materials, improper heat treatment, or missing/false markings. Current detection methods are either destructive, inconsistent, or too costly for scalable use. Our project aims to create a nondestructive, portable system that combines optical inspection with simple non-optical tests (weight, resistance, magnetism) to reliably detect counterfeit bolts. The system must remain lightweight, compact, and able to evaluate a bolt in minutes.

Proposed Design

Our design integrates optical imaging with a mechanical system to capture multiple bolt views while also measuring basic physical properties.

  • Optical Inspection: Bolts are placed on a motorized turntable, and two fixed cameras capture head markings, threads, and shank geometry under controlled lighting. Image data feeds into a computer vision pipeline, where a pretrained CNN extracts feature maps and an anomaly detection algorithm (PaDiM) compares them to authentic bolt distributions.
  • Non-Optical Tests: A digital scale measures weight, a magnetic probe detects alloy variations, and resistance is recorded to catch abnormal compositions. These values are compared against baselines established from authentic bolts.
  • Mechanical Structure: The setup uses T-slotted framing for modularity, stepper motors for bolt rotation, and Raspberry Pi–based control with external cameras. The entire system fits within a 12” × 12” × 12” cube and weighs under 12 lbs, meeting project constraints.

Future Work

  • Make device fully automated from bolt loading to image bolt detection.
  • Integrate real-time classification on the Raspberry Pi or a lightweight GPU board (e.g., Jetson).
  • Improve mechanical reliability of the turntable and screw-holding system for repeatable imaging.
  • Design a user interface that clearly outputs pass/fail results with traceable test data.
