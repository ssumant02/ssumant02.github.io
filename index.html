About Me

Hi! My name is Suchir Sumant, and I’m currently a Senior at the University of Illinois majoring in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science. I’m passionate about robotics and mechanical design, and I enjoy tackling hands-on engineering challenges. I am a part of the Illini Baja Offroad team and have interned at Navistar and General Motors over the past summers. I’m a motivated and driven individual with a strong desire to grow in the field, and I’m excited to apply my skills to real-world problems and contribute meaningfully to a team.
