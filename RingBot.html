The RingBot

Overview

RingBot is a motion-control platform exploring ring-shaped locomotion and stability. The system integrates embedded control, motor drivers, IMU feedback, and closed-loop trajectory tracking to evaluate multi-axis dynamics in a compact form factor.

Gallery

What

A ring-shaped mobile platform used to study balance, traction, and motion profiles across surfaces. Focus on modular hardware and reproducible tests.

How

Implemented microcontroller-based control, motor drivers, encoder feedback, IMU fusion for attitude estimation, and PID/trajectory controllers. Logged data via serial to Python for analysis and plots.

Results

Demonstrated stable step responses and repeatable path tracking. Identified control gains and friction conditions that minimize steady-state error and overshoot; established a baseline for future ML-assisted tuning.
